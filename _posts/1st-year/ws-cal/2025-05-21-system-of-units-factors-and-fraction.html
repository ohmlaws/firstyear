Q5: What is the LCM of 12, 18, 6, 36?

  • 12
  • 18
  • 36
  • 42
Show Calculation

Prime factorization:
12 = 2^2 \times 3,
18 = 2 \times 3^2,
6 = 2 \times 3,
36 = 2^2 \times 3^2

LCM = product of highest powers of all primes:
LCM = 2^2 \times 3^2 = 4 \times 9 = 36

Q7: What is the improper fraction for the given mixed fraction 7 \dfrac{3}{7}?

  • 20/5
  • 5/2
  • 4/3
  • 52/7
Show Calculation

To convert a mixed fraction to an improper fraction:

\[ 7 \dfrac{3}{7} = \dfrac{7 \times 7 + 3}{7} = \dfrac{49 + 3}{7} = \dfrac{52}{7} \]

Q9: Simplify: \frac{3}{4} + \frac{2}{5} - \frac{5}{20}

  • \frac{9}{10}
  • \frac{13}{10}
  • \frac{23}{20}
  • \frac{25}{20}
Show Calculation

Convert all terms to have denominator 20:

\[ \frac{3}{4} = \frac{15}{20},\quad \frac{2}{5} = \frac{8}{20},\quad \frac{5}{20} = \frac{5}{20} 

Now compute:
\[ \frac{15 + 8 - 5}{20} = \frac{18}{20} = \frac{9}{10} \]

Q10: Divide: \frac{20}{31} \div \frac{15}{62}

  • 2 \frac{4}{3}
  • 2 \frac{1}{3}
  • 2 \frac{3}{2}
  • 2 \frac{2}{3}
Show Calculation

\[ \frac{20}{31} \div \frac{15}{62} = \frac{20}{31} \times \frac{62}{15} 
\[ = \frac{1240}{465} = \frac{248}{93} 
Convert to mixed number:
\[ 248 \div 93 = 2 \text{ remainder } 62 \Rightarrow \frac{248}{93} = 2 \frac{62}{93} 
Since \frac{62}{93} \approx \frac{2}{3}, the final answer is:
\[ \boxed{2 \frac{2}{3}} \]
