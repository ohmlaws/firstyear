Q9: What is the \('x'\) value for \(x^2 + 6^2 = 10^2\)?

Show Calculation

Q12: What is the side AB if AC = 10 cm and BC = 6 cm?

  • 8 cm
  • 6 cm
  • 5 cm
  • 4 cm
Show Calculation

\[ AB = \sqrt{AC^2 - BC^2} = \sqrt{10^2 - 6^2} \] \[= \sqrt{100 - 36} = \sqrt{64} = 8\ \text{cm} \]

Q13: What is the side AB if BC = 15 cm and AC = 9 cm?

  • 4 cm
  • 8 cm
  • 10 cm
  • 12 cm
Show Calculation

\[ AB = \sqrt{BC^2 - AC^2} = \sqrt{15^2 - 9^2} \] \[= \sqrt{225 - 81} = \sqrt{144} = 12\ \text{cm} \]

Q14: What is the value of side AC if AB = 7 cm and BC = 5 cm?

  • 8.2 cm
  • 8.6 cm
  • 8.4 cm
  • 8.1 cm
Show Calculation

\[ AC = \sqrt{AB^2 + BC^2} = \sqrt{7^2 + 5^2} \] \[= \sqrt{49 + 25} = \sqrt{74} \approx 8.6\ \text{cm} \]

Q15: What is the length L₂, if total length (L) is 2.75 metres and L₁ : L₂ = 2 : 3?

  • 1.1 metre
  • 1.25 metre
  • 1.65 metre
  • 1.75 metre
Show Calculation

\[ \text{Total parts} = 2 + 3 = 5, \] \[\quad L_2 = \frac{3}{5} \times 2.75 = 1.65\ \text{metres} \]

Q16: How many days a mechanic takes to assemble 64 machines if he assembles 8 machines in 3 days?

  • 20 days
  • 22 days
  • 24 days
  • 26 days
Show Calculation

\[ \text{If 8 machines take 3 days}, \] \[\quad 1\ \text{machine takes } \frac{3}{8}\ \text{days} \] \[ \Rightarrow 64 \times \frac{3}{8} = 24\ \text{days} \]

Q17: What will be the RPM of smaller gear if a 180 mm dia meshes with 60 mm dia gear and the bigger gear makes 60 RPM?

  • 120 RPM
  • 140 RPM
  • 160 RPM
  • 180 RPM
Show Calculation

\[ \text{RPM}_{\text{small}} = \frac{180}{60} \times 60 = 180\ \text{RPM} \]

Q19: A motorcycle tyre is sold for ₹300. What is the purchase price if 25% profit is added?

  • ₹200
  • ₹220
  • ₹240
  • ₹260
Show Calculation

\[\text{Selling Price}= \text{Cost Price} + 25\%\ \text{of Cost Price} \] \[\Rightarrow 1.25x = 300 \Rightarrow x = \frac{300}{1.25} = 240 \]
