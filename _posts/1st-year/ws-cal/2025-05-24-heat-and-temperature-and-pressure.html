Q9: Convert 45°C into °F (Fahrenheit).

  • 110°F
  • 111°F
  • 112°F
  • 113°F
Show Calculation

\[ \frac{^\circ\text{C}}{5} = \frac{^\circ\text{F} - 32}{9}\]\[ °F = \frac{9}{5} \cdot 45 + 32 = 113°F \]

Q10: At what temperature do Fahrenheit and Celsius readings become equal?

  • -38°C
  • -39°C
  • -40°C
  • -41°C
Show Calculation

Set \(C = F\): \[\frac{^\circ\text{C}}{5} = \frac{^\circ\text{F} - 32}{9}\]\[ C = \frac{9}{5}C + 32 \Rightarrow -40 = -40 \]

Q11: Convert -273°C into Kelvin scale.

  • 0°K
  • 1°K
  • 2°K
  • 3°K
Show Calculation

\[ K = °C + 273 \Rightarrow -273 + 273 = 0\ K \]

Q12: What is the value in °C for 20°F?

  • -6.37°C
  • -6.47°C

Show Calculation

\[ °C = \frac{5}{9}(20 - 32) = \frac{5}{9} \cdot (-12) = -6.67°C \]
